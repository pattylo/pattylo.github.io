<div align="justify">
I'll be graduating as <strong>Master of Philosophy in August of 2024</strong>, and
with working thesis title, "Relative State Estimation for Non-inertial Control Framework", my research interest is broadly in robot state estimation with a focus on sensor fusion aided with machine learning.
This project is driven by the necessity to remotely control a UAV in an offboard, non-robocentric manner,
without the use of airborne sensors and computers. 
To determine the robot's state, we rely on on-ground cameras, transmitted control signals, and radar data, while employing factor graph optimization and machine learning backend smoothing.
